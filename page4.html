Pinturas Colom matte is a water based paint formulated with high level pigmentation and Vinyl-acrylic-tar-polymer. Pinturas Colom Satin

Pinturas Colom satin is a glossy water based paint suitable for interior and exterior wall and ceiling surfaces. It is particurlarly suitable for applying on construction materials, cement, gypsum,plaster. It is washable and resistant to harsh weather conditions.
